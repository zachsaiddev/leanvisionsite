Common Questions

FAQ

What do you do exactly?

We help you spot risks early and prevent crisis before it hits your bottom line.

What problems does this solve?

It cuts firefighting by giving you early warnings and clear ownership, so you can prevent productivity losses, rising costs, and cash strain before they escalate.

How quickly will we see results?

You'll usually get clearer visibility within weeks, then measurable improvement as actions are implemented.

What do you need from us to start?

Your key operational numbers (KPIs): ex. orders, delivery performance, labour/capacity, quality, stock/WIP, and basic finance.

Do we need new software?

Not always. We use what you have, and only add tools where automation or visibility is missing.

How do you prove ROI?

We constantly measure your key numbers — late orders, overtime, expediting, rework/scrap, stock/WIP, and cash collection — and convert the improvements into clear financial impact.
